Travel

Travel makes one modest, you see what a tiny place you occupy in the world.-Gustave Flaubert

I was lucky enough to attend a highschool that offered lots of travel oppurtunities and have parents who like to travel. Even though these trips are stressful, full of bug bites, sunburn and jetlag, the experiences are priceless. I would love to study abroad, but with Covid-19, I might have to put off those plans. Right now my top three places that I'd love to visit are: Norway (northern lights), Peru (Machu Picchu) and Egypt (Pyramids of Giza).
